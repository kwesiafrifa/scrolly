Individual-Focused

As a group, we wanted to find stories behind the data, and relied on words data the WFP recorded to do this.

Sample Map

Project Focus

We focused mainly on Nicaragua, El Salvador and Honduras, which were the three location sthe WFP respondent data covered.

We aim to focus on differences in outcomes of migrants to the US based on gender through the above lens.

Aim

We hope this will shed light on the motivations and reality of economic migrants to the United States, and that the focus on individual stories will help US residents empathize with their journeys.
